Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Do you allow walk-ins or reservations only?
    A: We accept both walk-ins and reservations. However, we recommend making a reservation to secure your spot, especially during peak hours.
  • Q: Are children allowed in the cafe?
    A: Yes, children are welcome! However, we kindly ask that children be supervised at all times to ensure the comfort of our cats and other guests.
  • Q: Do you serve food and drinks?
    A: Yes, we offer a variety of beverages and light snacks. Please note that outside food and drinks are not permitted.
  • Q: Are your cats vaccinated and well-cared for?
    A: Absolutely! The health and well-being of our cats are our top priority. All our cats are up-to-date on vaccinations and receive regular veterinary care.

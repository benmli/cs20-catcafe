About Us

We are a team of Kitty Lovers passionate about serving you! Our cafe combines our favourite things: Caffeine, Croissants and Cats. We combine the comforts of cats with warm pastries and beverages!
Come relax, pet some lovely kitties, and enjoy a yummy treat with a loved one.
